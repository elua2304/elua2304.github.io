Surrounded by expansive greenbelt and lush landscaping with stunning ocean views, Elua 2304 is a 2 bedrooms/2 baths condo located steps from the pristine waters of the famous Ulua Beach. Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, sunbathing, ocean views and more. Our condo offers the comforts of home with the amenities of a resort including WIFI, housekeeping, tennis, gym and pools.

Elua Village is a private gated property on 24 acres of beautiful tropical landscaping. Situated in the heart of Wailea’s resort community, we’re within walking distance to world class restaurants, shopping, spas and golf courses.
