Aloha from Maui!

Surrounded by expansive greenbelt and lush landscaping with stunning ocean views, Elua 2304 is a 2 bedrooms/2 baths condo located steps from the pristine waters of the famous Ulua Beach. Our condo was fully renovated in the fall of 2018 and offers the comforts of home along with resort level of services and amenities including WIFI, housekeeping, fitness center and two swimming pools.

Elua Village is a private gated property on 24 acres of beautiful tropical landscaping. Situated in the heart of Wailea’s resort community, we’re within walking distance to world class restaurants, shopping, spas and golf courses.

Amenities

Enjoy the comforts of home and the local lifestyle along with all the amenities of resort living! We hope you’ll take in the beauty of Maui from our home. Take advantage of our kitchen to cook a meal with fresh island ingredients and dine with the family on the lanai while enjoying the ocean view and beautiful sunset. Since this is a vacation, when you aren’t in the mood to cook enjoy fine dining at nearby world class restaurants such as Morimoto Maui, or enjoy a Hawaiian luau at Honua’ula (Grand Wailea) or Te Au Moana (Marriott).

Grab your swimsuits anytime and head down towards the beach. Within a couple of minutes you’ll be able to enjoy the sands and water of some of Maui’s finest beaches. Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, sailing and more at Ulua and Mokapu beach.

You’ll also be able to enjoy the two fabulous pools along with the hot tub. Get your exercise at the on-site gym and tennis court, take a stroll along the 2 mile Wailea Beach Walk or enjoy a round of golf are all within a few minutes walk.

When you’re ready to do some shopping, The Shops at Wailea is an essential stop and also within easy walking distance.

If you just want to relax and be pampered, spas are right next door provided by some of the world’s top resorts such as the Andaz Maui, Grand Walea (Waldorf Astoria Resort), Four Seasons Maui and Wailea Beach Resort (Marriott) and other premium hotels and restaurants and all accessible by foot.

You will have access to house keeping and concierge services provided through Ali’i Resorts.
